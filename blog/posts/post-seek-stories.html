Seek Stories Not Advice

25 January 2024

This one is fairly simple and short.
Most of the time advice is too generic to be helpful to you. Everyone's path is different and nuanced. When someone gives you advice, that's their distillation of a story they've experienced. Instead of getting the distallation, ask for the source -- the story. Rather ask people for their stories about X and draw your own conclusions from the story.
An added benefit is that humans evolved to remember stories better than we do advice.
